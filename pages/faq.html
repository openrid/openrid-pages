Open Regulatory Intelligence Database.

Detailed answer

openRID, which stands for "Open Regulatory Intelligence Database", is a modern and open resource designed to make regulatory knowledge accessible. Our database aggregates information from various organizations, bringing them together in a single, harmonized platform. openRID is your user-friendly, go-to tool for staying on top of evolving regulatory landscapes in medtech domain.
