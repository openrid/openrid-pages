At openRID, we help medtech organizations in navigating global regulations. We recognize that compliance can be complex, and we're here to provide businesses with a modern approach.

Our Vision

Our goal is to become the go-to provider of up-to-date regulatory intelligence, setting the industry standard for accessibility and reliability. We are dedicated to ensuring that all medical device and in-vitro medical device organizations, regardless of their size, can access essential regulatory information effortlessly. Our aim is to assist you in making well-informed decisions to not only achieve but also consistently maintain compliance with evolving regulatory standards.

What We Offer

Global Coverage

We collect regulatory intelligence data from various countries and regions, streamlining compliance for international businesses.

Harmonized Data

We harmonize data to create a user-friendly environment, simplifying search and ensuring data consistency.

Modern Approach

We leverage cutting-edge technologies for data aggregation and delivery, prioritizing a continually improved user experience.

Community

We ensure data accessibility for all, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing to collectively advance the industry.

Our Commitment

Our commitment goes beyond providing regulatory intelligence data. We strive to build a modern community of Regulatory Affairs specialists, industry professionals and compliance enthusiasts. Stay tuned for exciting developments on the horizon.

We look forward to supporting your compliance journey!
